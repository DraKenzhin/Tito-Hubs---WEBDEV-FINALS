Gunpla Grades Explained

HG (High Grade): Excellent detail and articulation for their size, a great starting point.

EG (Entry Grade): Simple to build, perfect for beginners and younger builders.

MG (Master Grade): Highly detailed, complex inner frame, and impressive articulation. For the enthusiast.

PG (Perfect Grade): The pinnacle of Gunpla, with intricate detail, complex engineering, and often LED compatibility.

RG (Real Grade): Focuses on realism with pre-molded inner frames and advanced articulation, offering a mid-range complexity with high detail.

Key Gunpla Timelines

Universal Century (UC): The original and most extensive timeline, featuring iconic mobile suits like the RX-78-2 Gundam and Zaku II.

Cosmic Era (CE): Known for its dramatic storytelling and mobile suits like the Freedom Gundam and Strike Gundam.

Popular Factions

Earth Federation: The primary human government in the Universal Century, often fielding advanced mobile suits.

Principality of Zeon: The main antagonist faction in the early Universal Century, famous for its distinctive mobile suits like the Zaku.

ZAFT (Zodiac Alliance of Freedom Treaty): A prominent faction in the Cosmic Era, known for its advanced mobile suit technology.
